The Endless Sands

Where silence speaks louder than words

In the cathedral of dunes,
time dissolves like morning mist.
Each grain holds a thousand years,
each wind carries ancient whispers.

The sun paints gold on amber waves,
shadows dance their evening waltz.
Here, solitude is not emptiness—
it is the space where souls expand.

Wanderer, rest your weary feet.
Let the warm sands embrace you.
In this vast and timeless sea,
find the peace you seek within.

"The desert tells a different story every hour."
