THE ART OF THE MIXTAPE

Before algorithms decided what you'd hear next, there was intention. Every song choice was deliberate. Every transition was crafted. You held the pause button at exactly the right moment, cutting silence like a surgeon. The mixtape was a message in a bottle, thrown into the sea of someone's Walkman.

RECORDED WITH LOVE

There's something pure about magnetic tape—how it captures not just sound, but feeling. The slight hiss between tracks is the sound of anticipation. The warble of an overplayed favorite is the mark of devotion. These imperfections are what made it human.
